Bio

Dr. P. Jakulin Divya Mary is a versatile academic professional with a diverse educational background. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, an MBA in HR & Marketing, and a Ph.D. in Management. As an Assistant Professor, she imparts her knowledge and expertise to students, fostering a passion for learning and research. Her accomplishments include clearing the National Eligibility Test (NET) for Lectureship and receiving the prestigious Maulana Azad National Fellowship. Dr. Divya Mary's dedication to research and teaching has made her an influential figure in the academic community, inspiring and guiding aspiring professionals in their academic and career pursuits.
